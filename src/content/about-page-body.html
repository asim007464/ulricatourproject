Our Story

Discover the true beauty of Jamaica with Ronica’s Splendid Tours, proudly operated by Ronicastours.com Limited and available online at Ronicas.com.

At Ronica’s Splendid Tours, comfort, safety, and authentic local experiences come together to create unforgettable journeys across Jamaica. We offer reliable airport transfers, private tours, and customized travel experiences designed to help you explore the island with confidence and ease.

Our tours are led by experienced, certified local drivers who know Jamaica beyond the guidebooks. From pristine beaches and hidden waterfalls to cultural landmarks and local favourites, we take you off the beaten path while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, transparency, and customer care.

Through Ronicas.com, visitors can easily learn about our services and book transportation and tours with confidence. With fixed prices, secure online booking, and modern, well-maintained vehicles, Ronica’s Splendid Tours removes the stress from travel so you can focus on enjoying your time on the island.

Whether you’re visiting Jamaica for the first time or returning to discover even more of its beauty, we are committed to making every journey smooth, safe, and memorable.

Travel with purpose. Travel with trust. Travel with Ronica’s Splendid Tours.

Operated by Ronicastours.com Limited.

Ronay Hadden - Certified JUTA Tour Driver

Ronay Hadden is a certified and highly experienced JUTA tour driver who has been proudly serving visitors since 2017. With years of hands-on experience navigating Jamaica’s roads and tourism industry, he is known for his professionalism, punctuality, and in-depth knowledge of the island’s history, culture, and hidden gems.

Ronay has earned recognition from TripAdvisor for consistently delivering outstanding service and memorable travel experiences. His friendly personality, commitment to safety, and attention to detail ensure that every guest feels comfortable, informed, and well cared for throughout their journey.

Whether providing airport transfers, private tours, or customized excursions, Ronay is dedicated to making every trip smooth, enjoyable, and authentic.
